Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 9, 2025 · Effective Date: June 9, 2025

🔒 Core Privacy Commitment:

ToolDuck.xyz operates entirely via client-side processing. No user documents, text strings, image files, log files, or any personal data payload are ever uploaded to, stored on, or processed by any remote server infrastructure. All computations occur exclusively within your local browser's JavaScript execution environment.

1. Who We Are

ToolDuck.xyz ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at toolduck.xyz (the "Platform"). We provide a collection of browser-based developer utility tools designed to help software developers, designers, and technical professionals process data locally on their own devices.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Because our tools operate entirely within your browser, the following categories of data are never transmitted to our servers:

  • Files you drag, drop, or select into any tool (images, logs, documents, etc.)
  • Text, code, JSON, YAML, JWT tokens, regex patterns, or any string data you paste into tool input fields
  • Cryptographic hashes, decoded outputs, or conversion results generated by any tool
  • File metadata, EXIF data, or document properties processed by our tools

All of the above data is processed exclusively in your device's browser memory (RAM) and is permanently discarded when you close the browser tab or navigate away.

3. Information We May Collect Automatically

Like most websites, our hosting infrastructure may automatically record standard server access logs when your browser requests page assets (HTML, CSS, JavaScript files). This may include:

  • Your IP address (typically anonymized or truncated)
  • Browser type and version (User-Agent string)
  • Referring URL
  • Date and time of the page request
  • Pages and resources requested

These logs are used solely for security monitoring, abuse prevention, and aggregate traffic analysis. They are never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Local Storage & Browser Cookies

We use your browser's localStorage API to store a small number of user preference values. These are stored entirely on your device and are never transmitted to our servers:

  • toolduck-theme: Stores your preferred color scheme
  • toolduck-cookie-consent: Records your response to our cookie consent notification

You can clear these at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or site data. We do not use session cookies for tracking purposes.

5. Third-Party Advertising — Google AdSense

We participate in the Google AdSense advertising program to support the continued free availability of this platform. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve advertisements to users based on prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet.

Specifically:

  • Google may use the DoubleClick cookie to serve ads across the ToolDuck.xyz platform and other websites
  • Google may use information about your visits to this site and other sites to provide personalized advertisements about goods and services
  • The use of advertising cookies enables Google and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet

Opt-Out: You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

Google's advertising privacy practices are governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

6. Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting, aggregated analytics (such as Google Analytics with IP anonymization enabled) to understand general usage patterns — which tool categories are most popular, which browser engines are most common, and so on. This data is aggregate and non-personally-identifiable. We do not use analytics to build individual user profiles or track specific user journeys across sessions.

7. GDPR — Rights of EEA Residents

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

  • The right to access personal data we hold about you
  • The right to rectification of inaccurate personal data
  • The right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • The right to restrict or object to processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our contact page. Because we collect minimal personal data by design, most requests can be fulfilled promptly.

8. CCPA / CPRA — Rights of California Residents

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected about them
  • Know whether their personal information is sold or disclosed, and to whom
  • Opt out of the sale of their personal information
  • Access their personal information
  • Request deletion of their personal information
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising privacy rights

We do not sell personal information. If you wish to exercise any CCPA/CPRA rights, contact us through the Contact page.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Platform is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove such information.

10. Data Security

Since the core value proposition of ToolDuck.xyz is that your data never leaves your device, the primary security boundary is your own browser and operating system. For the limited server-side infrastructure that delivers HTML, CSS, and JavaScript assets, we employ industry-standard TLS encryption, access controls, and regular security reviews.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy regularly. Continued use of the Platform after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Contact page. We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within 5 business days.
